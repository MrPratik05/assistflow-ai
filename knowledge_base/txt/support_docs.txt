Company: QuickHelp Store

Refund Policy:
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase if the product is unused, undamaged, and returned with the original receipt. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item is received.

Late Refunds:
If a refund has not appeared after 7 business days, customers should first check with their bank. If the issue continues, they should contact customer support with their order ID.

Shipping Policy:
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days. Express delivery takes 1 to 2 business days. International delivery may take 7 to 14 business days depending on the destination country.

Order Tracking:
Customers can track their order using the tracking link sent by email after dispatch. If the tracking link does not work, customers should contact support with their order number.

Damaged Items:
If an item arrives damaged, customers should contact support within 48 hours of delivery. They must provide photos of the damaged item and packaging.

Order Cancellation:
Orders can only be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once dispatched, customers must wait for delivery and then request a return.

Account Issues:
Customers who cannot log in should reset their password using the "Forgot Password" option. If they still cannot access their account, they should contact support with their registered email address.

Payment Issues:
If a payment fails, customers should check card details, available balance, and bank security approval. If money was deducted but the order was not confirmed, support should be contacted with payment reference details.

Human Support Escalation:
If the chatbot cannot answer a question, or if the customer asks for a human agent, the chatbot should politely escalate the issue to a human support representative.
